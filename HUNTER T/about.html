A BIT ABOUT US

TiGi Agency is a tourism agency located in the stunning town of Batumi, situated on the coast of the Black Sea in Georgia. It was founded by a couple of travel enthusiasts, X and Y, who shared a deep love for exploring new destinations and discovering new cultures. They started small, but with their dedication and hard work, TiGi Agency quickly became one of the most renowned tourism companies in the region. They offered a range of tours that catered to the diverse needs and interests of their clients, from sightseeing and cultural experiences to adventure sports and outdoor activities.

After years of travelling together and experiencing the wonders of the world, X and Y decided to pursue their passion and create their own tourism agency. They wanted to offer tourists a unique and authentic experience of Batumi and its surroundings, showcasing the beauty of Georgia to the world. One of the things that set TiGi Agency apart from other tourism companies was their personal touch. X and Y were passionate about creating custom tours for their clients, ensuring that each tour was tailored to their individual needs and preferences. They were also committed to providing exceptional customer service, going above and beyond to ensure their clients had the best possible experience. As their reputation grew, TiGi Agency expanded its team, bringing on board a group of experienced travel experts who shared their passion for adventure and discovery. Together, they worked tirelessly to create unique and unforgettable experiences for their clients, exploring the hidden gems of Batumi and its surroundings and sharing their knowledge and expertise with their guests. Today, TiGi Agency is a beloved institution in the town of Batumi, renowned for its exceptional service and commitment to providing authentic and memorable travel experiences. Thanks to the vision and dedication of Tiko and Giorgi, TiGi Agency has become a shining example of what can be achieved when passion and hard work are combined. Their love for travel and their commitment to showcasing the beauty of Georgia to the world continues to inspire and delight their clients, making TiGi Agency the go-to tourism agency for anyone looking for an unforgettable adventure in Batumi.
